Physical Activity by Occupation Given Gender

We can see here a chart the displays the average time people spend on physical activity given an occupation.
This chart provides user interactivity and can be filtered out by selecting the check box for male, female, or both.
When female is selected, we see the occupation that does the most physical activity is a doctor.
When male is selected, we see the occupation that does the most physical activity is an engineer.
When female and male is selected, we see the occupation that does the most physical activity is a nurse. Logically, we see that occupations that involve being on your feet for long period of time achieve the most physical activity.
