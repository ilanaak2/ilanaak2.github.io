Sleep Duration by Age

This chart shows information on the sleep duration average for varying ages of individuals.
We can see based on the coloring of the bar that 53 year olds have the highest sleep duration. 34 year olds have the lowest sleep duration.
We can observe here that on average individuals who are closer to the end of their careers sleep longer than individuals closer to the start of their careers.
This seems logical given that individuals may be speding more time advancing their careers at a younger age and may spend less time dedicated to work, therefore are able to sleep longer at the age of 53.
